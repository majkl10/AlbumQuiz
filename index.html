Album Guessing Game


How to Play

-Guess the rapper's album based on the blurred image.
-You have 5 tries to get it right.
-With each guess the image will be less blurry.
-If you can't guess it in 5 tries, the answer will be revealed.
-Click "Next" to move to the next album.

-If you guess 3 albums in a row correctly a streak will be activated.

Restart Game?

Are you sure you want to restart the game?

You will lose your current points.
